d. $\dfrac{d g_4(x)}{dx} = \dfrac{4(x^4+2x^2-x-3)(3x^2+1)}{(4x^3+4x-1)^2} = \dfrac{12x^6+28x^4-12x^3-28x^2-4x-12}{16x^6+32x^4-8x^3+16x^2-8x+1}$

​     when $x > 0$, $\vert g'_4(x)\vert < 1$. $g_4(1) = \dfrac{8}{7} > 1, g_4(2) = \dfrac{57}{39} < 2.$
